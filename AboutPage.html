Our Story

Built on 15+ Years of Engineering Credibility

PragmaApps was founded in 2007 with a single conviction: that great software is an outcome of engineering discipline, not headcount. We began by partnering with early-stage startups to build foundational platforms — systems that needed to survive scale, attract investors, and enter competitive global markets.

Over the next decade, our work expanded globally. We built and scaled products across the US, UK, Europe and the Asia-Pacific — working alongside CxOs, product teams and engineering leads at companies ranging from seed-stage startups to Fortune 500 enterprises. We helped several of these companies achieve successful exits and funding milestones.

In 2022, we made a deliberate pivot. We embedded AI into the core of every delivery workflow — from architecture to code review to deployment automation. The result was not just faster delivery. It was fundamentally better software, built with leaner teams, at a fraction of the legacy cost.

Today, PragmaApps is an AI-First digital engineering and consulting partner — combining the hard-won wisdom of 15+ years with the velocity of modern AI-assisted execution. We do not sell hours. We engineer outcomes.
