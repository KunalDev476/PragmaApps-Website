OUR PROCESS

How We Work

Software Built Faster — Without Cutting Corners

At Pragma Apps, AI does not replace engineering — it removes friction. We use AI to assist engineers in repetitive cognitive tasks so they can focus on architecture, correctness, and long-term reliability. The result is earlier clarity, better decisions, and fewer surprises.

01
🎯

Requirement Analysis & Scoping

Discover Missing Requirements Early

Many project delays originate at the very beginning: incomplete or ambiguous requirements. We use AI as a structured analysis assistant during discovery.

AI helps our team:

  • Transform informal business ideas into structured requirements
  • Generate user stories and acceptance criteria
  • Identify edge cases and failure scenarios
  • Surface hidden assumptions
  • Anticipate integration and data-flow issues
Why this matters

Traditional projects discover missing requirements during testing or after launch. We discover them during planning.

Impact
Clearer scope Accurate estimates Fewer change requests
02
🎨

Rapid Prototyping & UX Validation

Validate Workflows Before Building

Before writing production code, we validate workflows. AI accelerates prototyping so you see results faster.

AI helps us:

  • Explore alternate user flows
  • Draft UI structures
  • Simulate interaction scenarios
  • Prepare testable mock workflows
Early validation

Stakeholders can react to a working model early rather than reviewing static specifications.

Impact
Early feedback Reduced rework Higher adoption
03
⚡

Coding & Implementation

AI Co-pilot, Not Autopilot

During development, AI acts as an engineering co-pilot. AI accelerates execution, but engineering judgment remains human.

AI assists with:

  • Scaffolding APIs and modules
  • Repetitive data-handling code
  • Refactoring existing codebases
  • Understanding unfamiliar libraries
  • Documenting logic
Human oversight

Every change is still reviewed by engineers, tested, and integrated into a designed architecture.

Impact
Faster delivery Consistent code Focus on complexity
04
🔍

Code Reviews & Quality Assurance

Higher Coverage, Faster Cycles

Code review is one of the most time-consuming parts of software development. AI helps us review more thoroughly and generate comprehensive tests.

AI helps by:

  • Identifying potential logic errors
  • Flagging risky changes
  • Highlighting untested paths
  • Checking edge cases
  • Generating unit and integration tests
Thorough reviews

Engineers perform the final review and decide what is correct. AI helps us review code more thoroughly.

Impact
Higher test coverage Fewer regressions Faster releases
05
🔧

Bug Fixing & Troubleshooting

Faster Resolution, Shorter Downtime

Bug fixing is often slow because reproducing a problem is harder than solving it. AI helps analyze stack traces, logs, and error patterns quickly.

AI helps analyze:

  • Stack traces and error patterns
  • Logs and environment differences
  • Production anomalies
  • Abnormal behavior detection
  • Event correlation across services
Quick insights

Instead of manually searching through logs, engineers can quickly narrow the probable cause and validate hypotheses.

Impact
Faster debugging Shorter downtime Quick resolution

What AI Does — and What It Doesn't

AI helps with pattern recognition, reasoning assistance, and analysis. It does not make product decisions, architecture choices, or trade-offs. Those remain the responsibility of experienced engineers.

AI increases speed • Engineering maintains correctness

Behavioral Health & SaaS

CLIENT — LifeSherpa

8-Year Partnership Building Enterprise-Ready SaaS Platform from MVP

The Challenge

LifeSherpa needed to evolve from a basic MVP to an enterprise-grade platform. They required strict customer data separation, SSO support, business intelligence, web access for professionals, and scalable architecture — all while supporting thousands of active users.
